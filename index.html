About 1:7

(/wən-ˈsevən/)

What is 1:7?

1:7 is a network of specialized chat rooms focused on meaningful discussion of specific topics. We believe in creating spaces where people can engage in substantive conversations without the noise and distraction of general social platforms.

Our Philosophy

In an age of information overload, we value focused discourse. Each room in our network is dedicated to a specific subject area, allowing participants to dive deep into topics that matter to them.

Room Guidelines

All rooms follow our core principles:

- Respectful dialogue
- Evidence-based discussion
- Topic-focused conversations
- No spam or low-effort content
- Privacy and anonymity respected

Technical Details

Our platform is built on simple, open web technologies to ensure accessibility and longevity. We prioritize function over flashy features, creating spaces optimized for text-based conversation.
